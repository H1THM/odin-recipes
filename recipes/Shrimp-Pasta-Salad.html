Shrimp pasta salad

Description

A cool, vibrant, and satisfying dish perfect for picnics, potlucks, or summer meals. Tender pasta and succulent shrimp are tossed with crisp vegetables, fresh herbs, and a zesty dressing for a light yet flavorful meal.

ingredients

  • 4 oz (115g) pasta (penne, rotini, or shells)
  • ½ lb (225g) cooked shrimp (peeled & deveined)
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ small cucumber, diced
  • ¼ small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp fresh herbs (dill, parsley, or basil)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (or vinegar)
  • ½ small garlic clove, minced
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in boiling salted water until tender (8-10 mins). Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. Prep shrimp: If raw, sauté in oil for 2-3 mins until pink. Let cool.
  3. Chop veggies: Halve tomatoes, dice cucumber, slice onion.
  4. Make dressing: Whisk oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  5. Combine everything in a large bowl.
  6. Chill 15 mins (or up to 4 hours).
  7. Serve with extra herbs on top
