Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

creamy, protein-packed blend of ripe bananas, rich peanut butter, cocoa powder, and milk (or yogurt). Sweet, indulgent, and energizingâ€”perfect for breakfast or a post-workout treat. Ready in 5 minutes!

ingredients

  • Banana (frozen for thickness)
  • Peanut butter
  • Cocoa powder
  • Milk/yogurt
  • Honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Blend: Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Mix: Blend until smooth (30 sec). Add more milk if too thick.
  3. Taste: Adjust sweetness (add honey if needed).
  4. Serve: Pour into a glass. Top with banana slices or cocoa nibs.
